Our Story

BlockVest was born from a simple realization: the best wealth-building tools in history are locked out from over 80% of people. Not anymore, we are a movement to democratize wealth creation by making premium investment opportunities accessible to everyone.

Over the years, we watched talented, hardworking Nigerians struggle with a painful reality: no matter how much they saved, premium real estate remained out of reach. Meanwhile, the wealthy kept buying properties, earning 12-15% annual returns while the rest rented forever, building zero equity.

The wealth gap wasn't about work ethic or intelligence. It was about access.

What if we could break down a ₦50 million property into 500 pieces? What if anyone could own a fraction for just ₦100,000?

Fractionalization isn't new—it's how public companies work (stocks), how REITs operate, how the wealthy have always pooled capital. But it's never been accessible to everyday Nigerians. Until BlockVest.

Security & Compliance

  • • SEC-compliant investment platform
  • • KYC/AML verified investors
  • • Funds held in escrow until deal closes
  • • Bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL)
  • • Quarterly independent audits

Property Standards

Every BlockVest property must pass:

  • 1. Legal verification - Clear title, no disputes, proper documentation
  • 2. Physical inspection - Licensed surveyors assess condition and value
  • 3. Financial analysis - Projected returns validated by independent accountants
  • 4. Market validation - Location, demand, and growth potential verified
  • 5. Risk assessment - Insurance, tenant quality, exit strategy confirmed

If it doesn't pass all five, we don't list it.
