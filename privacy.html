PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Effective Date: January 15, 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

BlockVest Technologies Limited ("BlockVest," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information when you use our Platform.

This Policy complies with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 and other applicable data protection laws.

By using BlockVest, you consent to the data practices described in this Policy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information You Provide

Account Registration:

  • • Full name
  • • Email address
  • • Phone number
  • • Date of birth
  • • Residential address
  • • Nationality

Identity Verification (KYC):

  • • Government-issued ID (NIN, Driver's License, Passport, Voter's Card)
  • • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
  • • Selfie photograph
  • • Additional documents as required by law

Financial Information:

  • • Bank account details (for dividend payments)
  • • Payment card information (processed securely by payment partners)
  • • Investment history and preferences
  • • Source of funds documentation (for large investments)

Communications:

  • • Customer support inquiries
  • • Survey responses
  • • Newsletter preferences
  • • Feedback and reviews

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

Device and Usage Information:

  • • IP address
  • • Device type and operating system
  • • Browser type and version
  • • Pages visited and time spent
  • • Click patterns and navigation paths
  • • Referring website
  • • Dates and times of access

Cookies and Similar Technologies:

  • • Session cookies (required for Platform function)
  • • Analytics cookies (track usage patterns)
  • • Preference cookies (remember your settings)

See our Cookie Policy for details

Location Information:

  • • Approximate location based on IP address
  • • Precise location (only if you grant permission)

2.3 Information from Third Parties

Payment Processors:

  • • Transaction confirmation
  • • Payment status
  • • Fraud detection data

Identity Verification Services:

  • • ID verification results
  • • Risk scores
  • • Compliance checks

Public Sources:

  • • Property records
  • • Business registrations
  • • Sanctions lists

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information for:

3.1 Providing Services

  • • Creating and managing your account
  • • Processing investments and transactions
  • • Distributing dividends
  • • Providing customer support
  • • Sending service notifications and updates

3.2 Legal Compliance

  • • Identity verification (KYC/AML requirements)
  • • Preventing fraud and money laundering
  • • Complying with court orders and legal obligations
  • • Responding to regulatory requests

3.3 Platform Improvement

  • • Analyzing usage patterns
  • • Improving features and functionality
  • • Developing new services
  • • Testing and troubleshooting

3.4 Communication

  • • Sending investment opportunities
  • • Providing market updates
  • • Delivering newsletters (with your consent)
  • • Responding to inquiries

3.5 Security

  • • Detecting and preventing fraud
  • • Protecting against unauthorized access
  • • Monitoring for suspicious activity
  • • Enforcing our Terms of Service

3.6 Marketing (With Your Consent)

  • • Sending promotional offers
  • • Personalized recommendations
  • • Market research surveys

4. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

We process your personal information based on:

  • • Contract Performance: Necessary to provide our services
  • • Legal Obligation: Required by Nigerian law (KYC/AML)
  • • Legitimate Interest: Platform security, fraud prevention, improvement
  • • Consent: Marketing communications, optional features

5. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

We share your information with:

5.1 Service Providers

Payment Processors:

  • • Paystack (transaction processing)
  • • Banks (fund transfers)

Identity Verification:

  • • KYC/AML service providers
  • • Credit bureaus (for fraud prevention)

Technology Partners:

  • • Cloud hosting providers
  • • Analytics services
  • • Customer support tools

Professional Services:

  • • Legal advisors
  • • Accountants and auditors
  • • Compliance consultants

5.2 Regulatory Authorities

  • • Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC)
  • • Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS)
  • • Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC)
  • • Nigerian Financial Intelligence Unit (NFIU)
  • • Other regulatory bodies as required by law

5.3 Property-Related Parties

  • • Property management companies
  • • Legal representatives (for documentation)
  • • Insurance providers
  • • Other investors (for co-ownership purposes)

5.4 Business Transfers

If BlockVest is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred to the successor entity.

5.5 With Your Consent

We may share information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

6. DATA SECURITY

6.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

Technical Safeguards:

  • • 256-bit SSL encryption (data in transit)
  • • AES encryption (data at rest)
  • • Secure data centers with physical access controls
  • • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • • Intrusion detection systems

Organizational Safeguards:

  • • Employee access restricted on need-to-know basis
  • • Confidentiality agreements with staff
  • • Regular security training
  • • Incident response procedures

Account Security:

  • • Strong password requirements
  • • Two-factor authentication (available)
  • • Login alerts and activity monitoring

6.2 No Guarantee

While we implement strong security, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6.3 Your Responsibility

You must:

  • • Keep your password confidential
  • • Use secure devices and networks
  • • Log out after use on shared devices
  • • Report suspicious activity immediately

7. DATA RETENTION

7.1 Active Accounts

We retain your information while your account is active and for as long as necessary to provide services.

7.2 Closed Accounts

After account closure, we retain information for:

  • • 7 years: Financial records (regulatory requirement)
  • • 7 years: KYC documents (AML compliance)
  • • 5 years: Transaction history
  • • Indefinitely: Fraud prevention records

7.3 Deletion

After retention periods expire, we securely delete or anonymize your information.

8. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

Under NDPR, you have the right to:

8.1 Access

Request a copy of your personal information we hold.

8.2 Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.

8.3 Deletion

Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements).

8.4 Data Portability

Receive your information in a structured, machine-readable format.

8.5 Object to Processing

Object to processing based on legitimate interests (e.g., marketing).

8.6 Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent for processing that requires consent (doesn't affect prior processing).

8.7 Complain

Lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Bureau (NDPB).

8.8 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise these rights, email: privacy@blockvest.ng

We will respond within 30 days. We may request additional information to verify your identity.

9. COOKIES AND TRACKING

We use cookies and similar technologies. See our separate Cookie Policy for details.

  • • Essential Cookies: Required for Platform functionality (cannot be disabled)
  • • Analytics Cookies: Help us improve the Platform (can be disabled)
  • • Marketing Cookies: Deliver personalized ads (can be disabled)

Manage Preferences: Visit Settings > Privacy > Cookies

10. THIRD-PARTY LINKS

Our Platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their privacy policies before providing information.

11. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

BlockVest is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact privacy@blockvest.ng immediately.

12. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

Your information may be transferred to and stored in countries outside Nigeria where our service providers operate. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place.

13. AUTOMATED DECISION-MAKING

We may use automated processes to:

  • • Assess fraud risk
  • • Verify identity
  • • Recommend properties

You have the right to request human review of automated decisions affecting you.
