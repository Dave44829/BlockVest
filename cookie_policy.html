1. INTRODUCTION

This Cookie Policy explains how BlockVest ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website (www.blockvest.com) and mobile applications (collectively, the "Platform").

By using our Platform, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our use of cookies, you should adjust your browser settings or refrain from using our Platform.

2. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet, or other electronic device) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and provide information to the website owners.

Types of Cookies:

Session Cookies

Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. They help you navigate the Platform efficiently and remember information you've entered.

Persistent Cookies

Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They remember your preferences and actions across multiple visits.

First-Party Cookies

Set by BlockVest directly to help operate our Platform and understand how you use it.

Third-Party Cookies

Set by external services we use (like analytics providers or payment processors) to help us improve your experience and analyze Platform performance.

3. WHY WE USE COOKIES

BlockVest uses cookies for several important purposes:

Essential Functions

  • • Authenticating users and maintaining secure login sessions
  • • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • • Ensuring Platform security and preventing fraud
  • • Processing transactions and managing your investment portfolio

Performance and Analytics

  • • Understanding how visitors use our Platform
  • • Analyzing traffic patterns and user behavior
  • • Identifying technical issues and optimizing performance
  • • Testing new features and improvements

Personalization

  • • Tailoring content and investment opportunities to your interests
  • • Remembering your investment preferences and risk profile
  • • Providing customized dashboard views
  • • Enhancing your overall user experience

Marketing and Advertising

  • • Delivering relevant advertisements about our services
  • • Measuring the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • • Understanding which channels bring visitors to our Platform
  • • Retargeting visitors who have shown interest in our services

7. YOUR COOKIE CHOICES

You have several options to control or limit how cookies are used:

Cookie Consent Manager

When you first visit our Platform, you'll see a cookie banner allowing you to:

  • ✓ Accept all cookies
  • ✓ Reject non-essential cookies
  • ✓ Customize your cookie preferences by category

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

Google Chrome:

Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data

Mozilla Firefox:

Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data

Safari:

Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data

Microsoft Edge:

Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

Note: Blocking certain cookies may impact Platform functionality. Some features may not work properly if you disable essential cookies.

Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of targeted advertising cookies through:

  • • Google Ads Settings: adssettings.google.com
  • • Facebook Ad Preferences: facebook.com/ads/preferences
  • • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI): optout.networkadvertising.org
  • • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA): optout.aboutads.info
  • • European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance (EDAA): youronlinechoices.eu

Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. While we respect your privacy choices, our Platform does not currently respond to DNT signals, as there is no universal standard for how to interpret them. Instead, we encourage you to use our Cookie Consent Manager or browser settings to control your preferences.

Mobile Device Settings

For mobile applications:

iOS:

  • Settings > Privacy > Tracking (disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track")
  • Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

Android:

Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
