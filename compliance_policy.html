COMPLIANCE POLICY

1. INTRODUCTION

BlockVest is committed to maintaining the highest standards of regulatory compliance, investor protection, and ethical business practices. As a fintech investment platform offering fractional ownership across multiple asset classes, we operate within a comprehensive legal and regulatory framework designed to safeguard our investors and maintain market integrity.

This Compliance Policy outlines our commitment to adhering to all applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards.

2. REGULATORY FRAMEWORK

BlockVest operates in compliance with:

  • • Securities Regulations: All investment products offered through BlockVest comply with applicable securities laws and regulations in the jurisdictions where we operate.
  • • Financial Services Regulations: We adhere to financial services licensing requirements and maintain necessary registrations with relevant regulatory authorities.
  • • Consumer Protection Laws: We uphold consumer protection standards to ensure fair treatment of all investors.
  • • Data Protection Regulations: We comply with data privacy laws including GDPR, NDPR (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation), and other applicable data protection frameworks.

3. KNOW YOUR CUSTOMER (KYC) REQUIREMENTS

BlockVest implements robust KYC procedures to verify the identity of all investors:

Required Documentation

All investors must provide:

  • • Valid government-issued identification (National ID, International Passport, Driver's License, or Voter's Card)
  • • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement, or government correspondence dated within the last 3 months)
  • • Tax identification number or equivalent
  • • Source of funds declaration for investments above specified thresholds

Verification Process

  • • Automated identity verification using secure third-party services
  • • Manual review of documentation where required
  • • Enhanced due diligence for high-value investors
  • • Periodic re-verification as required by regulation

Account Approval

Investor accounts are activated only after successful completion of KYC verification. We reserve the right to request additional information or documentation at any time.

4. ANTI-MONEY LAUNDERING (AML) COMPLIANCE

BlockVest maintains a comprehensive AML program designed to prevent, detect, and report suspicious activities:

AML Measures Include:

  • • Customer Due Diligence (CDD): Risk-based assessment of all investors
  • • Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD): Additional scrutiny for high-risk investors or transactions
  • • Transaction Monitoring: Automated systems to detect unusual patterns or suspicious activities
  • • Sanctions Screening: Verification against global sanctions lists, PEP (Politically Exposed Persons) databases, and watchlists
  • • Reporting: Timely filing of Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) or Suspicious Transaction Reports (STRs) with relevant authorities

Red Flags We Monitor:

  • • Unusual transaction patterns inconsistent with investor profile
  • • Rapid movement of funds without clear investment purpose
  • • Attempts to structure transactions to avoid reporting thresholds
  • • Reluctance to provide required documentation
  • • Involvement of third parties without clear authorization

5. COUNTER-TERRORIST FINANCING (CTF)

BlockVest is committed to preventing the use of our platform for terrorist financing activities:

  • • Regular screening against terrorist watchlists and sanctions databases
  • • Monitoring for transactions that may indicate terrorist financing
  • • Cooperation with law enforcement and regulatory authorities
  • • Staff training on CTF indicators and reporting procedures

6. INVESTOR PROTECTION STANDARDS

Suitability Assessment

We assess investor suitability to ensure that investment products align with individual risk tolerance, investment objectives, and financial circumstances.

Risk Disclosure

All investment opportunities include comprehensive risk disclosures, ensuring investors understand:

  • • Nature of the investment
  • • Potential returns and associated risks
  • • Liquidity considerations
  • • Fees and charges
  • • Exit mechanisms

Fair Dealing

BlockVest is committed to:

  • • Treating all investors fairly and equitably
  • • Providing clear, accurate, and not misleading information
  • • Avoiding conflicts of interest, or disclosing them where they exist
  • • Handling investor complaints promptly and fairly

7. INVESTMENT LIMITS AND THRESHOLDS

To protect investors and comply with regulations:

  • • Minimum Investment: ₦500,000 or equivalent (subject to change)
  • • Investment Caps: Certain asset classes may have maximum investment limits based on investor classification and regulatory requirements
  • • Accredited vs. Non-Accredited Investors: Different investment opportunities and limits may apply based on investor status

8. FUNDS CUSTODY AND SECURITY

Custodian Arrangements

Investor funds are held in:

  • • Segregated client accounts with licensed custodian banks
  • • Trust accounts separate from BlockVest operational funds
  • • Secure escrow arrangements for specific transactions

Fund Protection

  • • Regular reconciliation and auditing of client accounts
  • • Insurance coverage for client assets where applicable
  • • Multi-signature authorization for fund movements
  • • Secure payment processing through licensed payment service providers

9. RECORD KEEPING AND REPORTING

BlockVest maintains comprehensive records including:

  • • Investor account information and transaction history (minimum 7 years)
  • • KYC/AML documentation
  • • Investment contracts and agreements
  • • Communication records with investors
  • • Compliance monitoring and audit reports

Reporting to Investors

Investors receive:

  • • Transaction confirmations
  • • Quarterly portfolio statements
  • • Annual tax documentation
  • • Performance reports on invested assets

Regulatory Reporting

We submit required reports to regulatory authorities including:

  • • Periodic compliance reports
  • • Transaction reporting where required
  • • Suspicious activity reports
  • • Audit reports and financial statements

10. STAFF TRAINING AND OVERSIGHT

Compliance Training

All BlockVest staff undergo regular training on:

  • • Applicable laws and regulations
  • • KYC/AML/CTF procedures
  • • Data protection and privacy
  • • Ethical conduct and conflict of interest
  • • Investor protection standards

Compliance Officer

BlockVest has designated a Chief Compliance Officer responsible for:

  • • Overseeing compliance program implementation
  • • Monitoring regulatory changes
  • • Conducting internal compliance audits
  • • Serving as liaison with regulatory authorities
  • • Managing compliance-related inquiries and investigations

11. THIRD-PARTY DUE DILIGENCE

BlockVest conducts thorough due diligence on:

  • • Property Developers and Asset Originators: Verification of credentials, track record, and financial stability
  • • Service Providers: Assessment of technology vendors, payment processors, and custodians
  • • Partners and Affiliates: Regular review of business relationships
  • • Continuous Monitoring: Ongoing assessment of third-party compliance and performance

12. CYBERSECURITY AND TECHNOLOGY COMPLIANCE

Security Measures

  • • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • • Multi-factor authentication for investor accounts
  • • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • • Incident response and business continuity plans
  • • Compliance with relevant cybersecurity frameworks

Technology Standards

  • • Secure development practices
  • • Regular system updates and patch management
  • • Access controls and audit logging
  • • Data backup and disaster recovery procedures

13. MARKETING AND ADVERTISING COMPLIANCE

All BlockVest marketing materials:

  • • Provide fair, clear, and not misleading information
  • • Include appropriate risk warnings
  • • Comply with advertising standards and regulations
  • • Avoid making guarantees or promises of specific returns
  • • Are reviewed and approved by compliance before publication

14. CONFLICT OF INTEREST MANAGEMENT

BlockVest identifies, manages, and discloses conflicts of interest:

  • • Transparent disclosure of ownership structure
  • • Clear separation between investor interests and company interests
  • • Independent valuation of assets where required
  • • Fair allocation of investment opportunities
  • • Restrictions on employee personal trading

15. COMPLAINT HANDLING AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Complaint Process

Investors can submit complaints through:

  • • Email: compliance@blockvest.com
  • • Online portal: Investor dashboard complaint section
  • • Written correspondence to our registered office

Resolution Timeline

  • • Acknowledgment within 2 business days
  • • Investigation and response within 14 business days
  • • Escalation to senior management if unresolved
  • • Access to alternative dispute resolution mechanisms

Regulatory Escalation

If not satisfied with our resolution, investors may escalate to:

  • • Relevant financial ombudsman or regulatory authority
  • • Consumer protection agencies
  • • Legal remedies as provided by law

16. REGULATORY EXAMINATIONS AND AUDITS

BlockVest cooperates fully with:

  • • Regulatory examinations and inspections
  • • External audits and reviews
  • • Law enforcement investigations
  • • Court orders and legal processes

We maintain systems to respond promptly to regulatory inquiries and produce required documentation.

17. ONGOING COMPLIANCE MONITORING

Internal Controls

  • • Regular compliance audits and risk assessments
  • • Automated monitoring systems for transaction patterns
  • • Periodic review of policies and procedures
  • • Testing of compliance program effectiveness

Continuous Improvement

We regularly update our compliance program to reflect:

  • • Changes in applicable laws and regulations
  • • Emerging risks and industry best practices
  • • Lessons learned from compliance monitoring
  • • Feedback from regulators and investors

18. WHISTLEBLOWER PROTECTION

BlockVest encourages reporting of potential compliance violations:

  • • Protected channels for anonymous reporting
  • • Non-retaliation policy for good faith reports
  • • Investigation of all reported concerns
  • • Confidentiality protection for whistleblowers

Report compliance concerns to: ethics@blockvest.com

19. SANCTIONS AND ENFORCEMENT

Consequences of Non-Compliance

Failure to comply with our policies may result in:

  • • Account suspension or termination
  • • Rejection of transactions
  • • Reporting to regulatory authorities
  • • Legal action where appropriate

Investor Obligations

Investors must:

  • • Provide accurate and complete information
  • • Update KYC information when circumstances change
  • • Comply with platform terms and conditions
  • • Use the platform for legitimate investment purposes only

20. UPDATES TO THIS POLICY

BlockVest reserves the right to update this Compliance Policy to reflect:

  • • Changes in applicable laws and regulations
  • • Evolution of our business and product offerings
  • • Enhancement of compliance procedures
  • • Regulatory guidance and industry standards

Material changes will be communicated to investors via email and platform notifications.
