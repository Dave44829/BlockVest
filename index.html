    BlockVest is Nigeria's leading fractional real estate investment platform that democratizes property ownership by allowing you to invest in premium real estate starting from just ₦100,000.

    How It Works:

    We acquire high-value properties in prime locations and divide ownership into affordable blocks. Each block represents a legal fractional share of the property. When you purchase blocks, you become a proportional owner with full legal rights to rental income and property appreciation.

    BlockVest investors typically earn returns through two primary channels: Rental Income and Property Appreciation. While Residential Properties are estimated to yield 8-12% annually and Property Appreciation 8-15% annually, these are just estimates and not fixed yields - actual result may vary.

    Real estate investing carries risks like market flunctuation, vacancy, tenant default, and economic changes. To mitigate these risks, we conduct thorough property checks, offer professional management, provide insurance, encourage portfolio diversification, and offer transparent reporting, all backed by legal protection.

    You will receive quarterly payouts of rental income and appreciations minus expenses. Our fee is 5% of earnings, with no hidden charges. We handle property management, maintenance and reporting with transparent breakdowns via email and dashboard.

    Your investment is protected through bank-level security, quarterly audits, and most importantly legal ownership of properties. Even if BlockVest were to close, your ownership would remian valid, with options to hire a new manager, sell, or transfer. Additional protections include seperate bank holding, insurance, government oversight, and legal rights to sue. Your protection comes from legal ownership, not just trust in BlockVest
